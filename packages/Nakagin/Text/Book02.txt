<P>4.) Wombat Poop is Cube-Shaped
<P>The digestive process of wombats creates cube shaped poop. This prevents the 
<P>feces from rolling a way, and is used as a way to mark territory, and attract mates.
<P>
<P>5.) Penguin Poop Can Be Seen From Space
<P>Penguin colonies produce so much poop that it can be detected by satellites, helping
<P>scientists track their population.
<P>
<P>6.) Some Animals Use Poop as a Defense Mechanism
<P>The bombardier beetle can eject a hot, noxious chemical spray from its abdomen, which
<P>is essentially toxic poop, to deter predators.
